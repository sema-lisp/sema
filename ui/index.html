Page
The root wrapper — it provides the theme tokens (--bg, --gold, …), loads the brand fonts, and applies the body reset. It has no visible box of its own (display:contents) and owns global document.body styles, so it wraps the app once rather than nesting — this very page is wrapped in one.
usage
<!-- full-height: pins body to 100vh, hides overflow --> <!-- flex: makes body a column flexbox --> <sema-page full-height flex> <sema-container size="md"> <!-- your app --> </sema-container> </sema-page>
